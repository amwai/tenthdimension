Alex Mwai, Founder & CEO

Alex has more than ten years experience extracting actionable insights from extremely large and complex datasets. Before turning to data science, Alex was a nuclear physicist applying methods in advanced analytics and distributed computing to particle collision datasets so as to understand the transitions of matter into energy. Today, he is helping organizations across different sectors discover the gems of insights hidden in their data that will help them meet their business objectives. Alex completed his PhD in high-energy nuclear physics from the State University of New York at Stony Brook.
